Bobby Hackett - Creole Cookin'


Exclusive - first ever digital release!!!

I am extremely proud to release the first digital version of the long out-of-print 1967 Dixieland jazz album - Creole Cookin' - by cornetist Bobby Hackett, in Pure DSD!
This album features tenor great Zoot Sims, and is considered "essential" and "one of [Hackett's] finest all-around recordings", rated 4.5 stars by Scott Yanow of AllMusic.
